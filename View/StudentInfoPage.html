ABOUT PROJECT




Assumptions

  • The system is modelled to capture information of students in a tertiary institution.
  • Modification of student details can cause a change in his/her matriculation number, since it depends on the department (which depends on the faculty) and level of the student.
  • The system is to be used for only viewing, addition, modifcation and deletion of student information.
  • All faculties, departments and years of study are generically modelled.

Limitations

  • The data available is limited to only that which is accepted by the system and may not have all the content that could be found in a regular tertiary institution.
  • The system needs javascript enabled by the client/browser to activate its functionality.

Frameworks Used

  • MongoDb: To store student information, as well as other relevant data.
  • Express: To act as a server/middleware which transfers content to the client.
  • JQuery: To perform DOM elements manipulation, make ajax calls to the web server and enhance interactivity of the system.
  • Node: To handle server side functionality
